To my Penguin

For the most beautiful person in my life.

Chapter One

Our Story

Before you, my days simply happened — one after another, unremarkable. Then somehow, in the ordinary way that the most extraordinary things arrive, you appeared. And every day since has had a different color to it.

Distance tried to test us. Miles sat between us more nights than I can count, yet somehow you never felt far. A voice note at midnight, a "good morning" sent before either of us were fully awake — it was enough to make an ocean feel like a hallway.

Every conversation mattered, even the small ones about nothing at all. Every single smile you sent me — through a screen, half-lit at 2am, still managed to fix whatever kind of day I was having.

Seven years later, I am still choosing you. Still learning you. Still grateful that out of every direction my life could have gone, it went toward yours.

Chapter Six

Thank You

Thank you for being patient with me — with my flaws, my busy weeks, my imperfect way of showing love.

Thank you for choosing me, again and again, when you didn't have to.

Thank you for supporting me quietly, in ways I probably never fully noticed.

Thank you for believing in me on days I stopped believing in myself.

And thank you, simply, for loving me. Seven years of it, and it still feels like a gift I haven't earned.

Chapter Seven

A Small Apology

My Penguin,

I need to say something honest first. I'm deeply sorry that I couldn't finish this for you on July 1st — our actual anniversary. I wanted so badly to hand you something the moment the day arrived, something that showed you exactly how much these seven years have meant.

Life got busy in the way it sometimes does, and I ran out of hours before I ran out of things I wanted to say to you. I know that doesn't undo the wait.

But I need you to know — not a single day did I forget our anniversary. Not a single moment did I stop loving you while I was building this, page by page, late at night, trying to get it right.

This website may be late. But my love never was, and never will be.

Thank you for understanding me, even now. I promise to keep trying — to be better, to show up sooner, to love you louder. And I promise, always, to choose you.

Love,
Zaid

Chapter Eight

My Penguin ❤️

You are my peace on the days that feel too loud, and my happiness on the days that don't need fixing at all.

You are my favorite notification — the one name I look for before any other, every single time my phone lights up.

You are my comfort, my home even from far away, and somehow, already, my future.

If I were given another lifetime, I would spend it exactly the same way — looking for you, and choosing you the moment I found you. Distance means nothing when someone means everything, and you have always meant everything.

Thank you for every smile you've given me, every call that ran too long, every message that made my whole day, every laugh I could hear even through text.

I promise to stay. I promise to protect your heart the way you've always protected mine. And I promise to never stop loving you — not after seven years, not after seventy.

Forever yours,
Zaid ❤️
